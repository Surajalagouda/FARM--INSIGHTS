ABOUT US

We are moving ahead with an innovative idea of providing ready to use and easy to use Soil pH testing kits. From these kits user can analyze pH of their soil without any external help. Then they can use our website or guiding pamphlets for selection of crops. user can also get current and assumed market prices. The kit contains testing equipment's which include test tubes, to put in the mixture of water and the reagent and a measuring test tube for water input . Along with testing equipment, it comes with an instruction leaflet, a plant pH preference list and QR code to visit our website for more information regarding crop selection using the pH test results.
